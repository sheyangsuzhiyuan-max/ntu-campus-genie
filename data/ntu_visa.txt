【文档标题】国际学生签证申请指南 (Student's Pass Application)

1. 什么是 STP (Student's Pass)?
1 (EN): What is STP (Student's Pass)?
所有国际学生在新加坡全日制学习必须持有 ICA 颁发的学生准证 (STP)。
All international students studying full-time in Singapore must hold a Student's Pass (STP) issued by ICA.

2. 申请流程 (SOLAR 系统)
2 (EN): Application Process (SOLAR System)
- 收到 NTU 的录取通知书 (Offer Letter) 后，学校会给你一个 SOLAR Application Number。
  After receiving NTU's admission offer letter, the school will give you a SOLAR Application Number.
- 登录 ICA 官网的 SOLAR 系统 (Student's Pass On-Line Application & Registration)。
  Log in to ICA's SOLAR system (Student's Pass On-Line Application & Registration).
- 填写 Form 16 和 Form V36。
  Fill in Form 16 and Form V36.
- 支付 $30 处理费。
  Pay $30 processing fee.

3. 体检要求 (Medical Check-up)
3 (EN): Medical Examination Requirements
- 所有申请 STP 的学生必须进行 HIV (艾滋病) 和 X-ray (肺结核) 检查。
  All STP applicants must undergo HIV and X-ray (tuberculosis) screening.
- 建议直接在 NTU 校医院 (Fullerton Health) 做体检，结果会直接上传给 ICA，省去跑腿麻烦。
  Recommended to do medical check-up at NTU's clinic (Fullerton Health), results will be uploaded directly to ICA, saving you the hassle.
- 体检费用：约为 $50 SGD。
  Medical check-up cost: Approximately SGD 50.

4. 领取证件 / Collecting Student's Pass
- 在抵达新加坡并完成体检后，需预约去 ICA 大楼 (Lavender MRT 附近) 进行指纹录入和领取实体卡。
- After arriving in Singapore and completing medical check-up, book appointment at ICA Building (near Lavender MRT) for fingerprint enrollment and card collection.
- 必须携带材料 / Required documents:
  • 护照 / Passport
  • IPA Letter (批准信) / IPA Letter (Approval Letter)
  • 体检报告 / Medical examination report
  • 护照照片 / Passport-sized photos
